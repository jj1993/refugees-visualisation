Refugees in Europe

An educated view on the European migrant crisis

The European migrant crisis is all over the news, but how these migration flows developed over the last years is largely unknown. From what countries do these refugees come, and how are the countries outside of Europe affected? This website is designed to give an easy and clear insight into the data and statistics on refugees all over the world. Don't forget to try the tab More data on refugees!
